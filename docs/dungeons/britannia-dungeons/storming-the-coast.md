Storming the Coast is the first of the new Britannia Dungeons.

Entry level: **120**

Dungeon points to complete: **12**

Level on which you can complete: **120**

Enemy and boss levels: **134-136**  
  
Minions item level drop: **132-138**  
  
Boss item level drop: **135-144**  

Parent Expedition: [Bank of the Thames](/expeditions/britannia-expeditions/bank-of-the-thames "Bank of the Thames")

Advanced Dungeon: **No**

Previous Dungeon: [Alpha & Omega](/dungeons/germania-dungeons/alpha-omega "Alpha & Omega") (Level 120)

Next Dungeon: [The Corrupted World Tree](/dungeons/britannia-dungeons/the-corrupted-world-tree "The Corrupted World Tree") (Level 135)

## Description

The Roman landing barges reach the shore, but Britannia’s coasts are rocky, and the hinterlands forested and easily defended. Troops of Britons, under the leadership of Dubnovellaunus, wait to ambush the invaders. Prepare your soldiers for the first deadly attack – the war starts here!

![Storming the Coast](https://gladiatusfansite.blob.core.windows.net/images/Dungeons/Britannia/storming-the-coast/storming-the-coast-first-screen.png "Storming the coast")

### Special abilities

#### Round 1, Round 11, Round 21 (every 10 rounds)

`Dubnovellaunus channels all his strength with unswerving focus. All enemy attacks break harmlessly over his steely determination, and are reflected back at the attacker with devastating force.`

All attacks against him this round do 0 damage.

#### Round 2, Round 10, Round 18 (every 8 rounds)

`Dubnovellaunus brings his weapon crashing down with brutal force. His victim stumbles back, stunned, seeing nothing but stars as their consciousness fades.`

Some mercenaries do not attack at all this round. Neither is Dubnovellaunus.

#### Round 3, Round 8, Roumd 13 (every 5 rounds)

`Whirling around with brute force, Dubnovellaunus deals a sweeping attack which downs his enemies in a shower of splintering metal and bone.`

Does AoE damage to the entire dungeon party
