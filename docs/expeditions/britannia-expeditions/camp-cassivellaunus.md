## Description

Galerius Senecianus\` Diary: DCXCIX a.u.c. We managed to put the moor behind us. My shoes and those of my companions are soaked to the core from sinking in the quagmire. Our commander Gaius Trebonius... not so much. To be quite honest, there is something about him that seems off to me.
