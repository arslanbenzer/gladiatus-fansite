Upgrades are items that permanently increase a certain stats when applied to an item. The higher the item level of the upgrade item the higher the stats it will increase. Most upgrade items have a minimum item level that they can be applied to so be aware of it. Also, if you are buying an Upgrade item from the Market, make sure it's not Soul bound to the seller because if it is, you will not be able to use it.

## Dusts

Dusts are temporary 1 hour buffs that increase one of your main character stats for a period of time. Dusts cannot be purchased from Auction house or vendors and only drop from mobs. Please note that these do not exceed the maximum stat of your character, so if you are at the maximum stat this will not have any effect

## Protective Gears

Protective gears are permanent armour boost that can be applied to all slots except weapons and jewellery (amulet and rings). Please note that all of the protective gears have a minimum level of the item.

### Protective gear

<ProtectiveGear level={112} />

Permanently increases armor on Armour slots. Not usable on amulets, rings and weapons. Beware of the minimum level requirement

Formulas:

- Armour = Item level of the Protective gear * 2 (round up). Example above, Item level 112 * 2 = 224 Armour
- minimum item level to use on = Item level of the Protective gear - 5. Example above, Item level 112 - 5 = 107
- Rarity multipliers for items (green, blue, purple, orange, red) apply to the +Armour but not for gold value

## Grindstones

Grindstones are weapon damage enchants and protective gear is an upgrade for Armour which increases the armor points

### Grindstone

<Grindstone level={97} />

Permanently increases damage on weapons. Beware of the minimum item level. Usable only on weapons.

Formulas:

- Damage = Item level of the grindstone / 5 (round up). Example above, Item level 97 / 5 = 19.4, rounded up to 20 damage increase
- minimum item level to use on = Item level of the grindstone - 5. Example above, Item level 97 - 5 = 92
- Rarity multipliers for items (green, blue, purple, orange, red) apply to the +Damage but not for gold value
